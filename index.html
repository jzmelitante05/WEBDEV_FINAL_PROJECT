Deep Into The Grind

It started with one machine and a dream. In early 2026, founders Castro, Macadagdag, Matacot, Melitante, and Pajimola converted a small garage space into a roasting lab, armed with a single drum roaster and a secondhand espresso machine.

Word spread fast. Neighbors started lining up at 7 AM for a cup of the house blend — a medium-dark roast with notes of dark chocolate and dried cherry. Within six months, The Grindscape had outgrown the garage and moved to its current home on Marcos Highway.

Today, we hand-roast our beans in small batches every single morning. We source directly from farming cooperatives in Benguet and Sagada — ensuring fair wages and traceable supply chains. Every cup you drink here puts money back into the hands of Filipino farmers.
